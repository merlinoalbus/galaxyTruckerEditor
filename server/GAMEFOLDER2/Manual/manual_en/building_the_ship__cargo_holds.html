Cargo Holds

Cargo holds come with 2 or 3 containers. They store the goods you find along the way.

Of course, goods translate into profits, which is what this journey is all about, so you want as many cargo holds as possible.

At the end of your journey, you gain the number of cosmic credits indicated by the price list:

Hazardous Goods Containers

Red goods are the most valuable, but they are hazardous materials. They can be transported only in special reinforced containers – the red ones. Cargo holds with red containers usually have only 1 or 2 containers. The good news is that a red container can hold goods of any color.

Red goods cannot go in white containers. Any goods can go in red containers.

Red goods are the most valuable, so it is important to have a lot of cargo holds with red containers.
