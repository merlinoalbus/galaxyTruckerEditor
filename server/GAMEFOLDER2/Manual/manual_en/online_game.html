Online Game

Online Account

If you want to play an online game, you will need a Galaxy Trucker online account. Click on the inputbox in the upper left part of your cockpit near your avatar.


The green dot by your online account means that you are connected to the server. Click it if you want to change something or to disconnect.

Online Game Lobby

In the main menu, click "Multiplayer" and then click "Online Game". This will take you to the Online lobby.



The right side of the screen lists games you can join. Clickping the game brings you the game details in the left part of the screen.



In the quick view and in the game details, you can see the game title and settings:

Building mode: Real-Time game or Turn-Based game.
Game speed: How much time you have for your turn and reserve time for the entire game.
How many, and which flights the game consists of.
What deck of adventure cards will be used.
How much control the players have over the flight.

Clickping an icon in the game details window will bring you more info about each setting.


There is also the list of players and free slots. The number above the player shows the rank and the colored icon below his or her online status:

  • The player is in the lobby and ready to play.
  • The player has the app running, but he or she is not in the lobby right now.
  • The player does not have the app running

You can join the game by clicking the "Join Game" button. If you change your mind, you can leave the game before it starts by clicking the "Leave Game" button.

Note: You can play only one real-time game at a time and you have to be ready when the game starts. Leaving the lobby means leaving the real-time game. However, you can play any number of turn-based games at once.

Create a New Game

If you want to start a new game yourself, just click the New Game button in the upper right corner.


You can choose a name for the game and set the options mentioned above.

You can also set the number of players. The first two slots are mandatory (there will always be you and at least one other player). You may set the other slots to "required" or "closed". They are "optional" by default - other players may join the game, but the game can also be started without them.

Once you click "Create Game", the game will be added to the list of the games - the other players will see it and may join it. You cannot change the settings of this game anymore.

Only the player that created the game may start it (by clicking the "Start Game" button) - or delete it instead ("Delete Game").

List of Your Games

The upper part of the lobby screen lists all your active games. If it's your turn, you see a red exclamation mark in that game and there is a countdown too. If the countdown is red, your time for the turn has run out and now your time for the whole game is at stake. If it runs out too, you will be removed from the game and the AI will replace you.

You can see the game details by clicking the game and you can enter the game by clicking the "Load Game" button.

Finished games are grey. They will disappear after you see the game results.

If you don't want to continue the game, please be respectful to other players - enter the game for the last time and click "Give up" in the menu. This way the others don't have to wait until your time runs out.

Results Screen

After each online game, you will see the results screen, where you can see how many experience points you gained:

If you earned at least one credit, you'll gain a nice sum of experience points.
If you had zero credits, you'll still gain some points for finishing the game.
If you are replaced by AI (whether you gave up or you had no time left), you gain no experience points.
If you earned more than other players, you'll gain experience points according to their ranks. Defeating higher-ranked players earns you more points.
If you fly with higher ship classes or you fly more flights, your experience is multiplied!

Ranks

After the game is over, you gain some experience points. If you have enough experience points, you will be promoted to a higher rank!


Hint: In the lower ranks, you get enough experience just by playing the game. However, in the higher ranks, you will need much more experience points and the best way to get it is to defeat players with high ranks.
