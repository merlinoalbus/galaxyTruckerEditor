SCRIPT ship_random_Dlg

  ShowDlgScene
  ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
  ShowChar tutor left
  Delay 400
  Say "Sorry. I'm not doing tutorials right now."
  Delay 500
  ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor-pizza.png
  Delay 500
  Ask "It's my lunch break."
  MENU
    OPT_IF tutor_somemissions "Don't you recognize me?"
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor-smile.png
      Ask "Hey! It's my little newbie! What are you doing here?"
      MENU
        OPT "Just checking out this new expansion."
          GO show_expansion_stuff
        END_OF_OPT
      END_OF_MENU
    END_OF_OPT
    OPT "I'm not here for a tutorial. I want to see the new stuff in the expansion."
      GO show_expansion_stuff
    END_OF_OPT
  END_OF_MENU

  LABEL show_expansion_stuff
  Delay 300
  ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor-smile.png
  Delay 200
  Say "Oh, you'll like this!"
  Say "The Corp Inc engineers have developed a revolutionary new ship component."
  Ask "And they want you to test this unconventional ship design."

  LABEL ship_random_ask
  MENU
    OPT_IFNOT askedHowUnconventional "So ... just how unconventional is this ship design?"
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor-smile.png
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Ask "Well, it's so unconventional that you won't know what shape it is until you start building."
      SET askedHowUnconventional
      SET showOptCommentOnRandomDesign
      GO ship_random_ask
    END_OF_OPT
    OPT_IF showOptCommentOnRandomDesign "Are you telling me that this new ship design is completely random?"
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Say "Of course not!"
      Say "It's based on an incredibly sophisticated evolutionary algorithm that takes into account the gravitonic pressure of the 18 nearest pulsars, as well as quantum fluctuations in the stock market."
      Ask "Some of the finest minds in sanitation engineering worked on it. Took 'em all night to get it right."
      RESET showOptCommentOnRandomDesign
      GO ship_random_ask
    END_OF_OPT
    OPT_IFNOT askedTellNewComponent "Tell me more about this \"revolutionary\" new component."
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Say "It's a cargo hold that can also hold batteries."
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Ask "... Or maybe it's a battery component that can hold cargo."
      SET askedTellNewComponent
      SET showOptCommentOnCargoBattery
      GO ship_random_ask
    END_OF_OPT
    OPT_IF showOptCommentOnCargoBattery "So the revolutionary new component is just a cargo hold?"
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Say "It's a cargo AND battery hold. Weren't you listening?"
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor.png
      Ask "Maybe it doesn't sound like much, but it'll let you cram more stuff onto your ship. And that's always good."
      RESET showOptCommentOnCargoBattery
      GO ship_random_ask
    END_OF_OPT
    OPT "Sounds good. Let's go."
      ChangeChar tutor campaign/tutor-pizza.png
      Say "Good luck, newbie."
      HideDlgScene
      RETURN
    END_OF_OPT
  END_OF_MENU
  HideDlgScene
  RETURN
  RETURN
END_OF_SCRIPT
