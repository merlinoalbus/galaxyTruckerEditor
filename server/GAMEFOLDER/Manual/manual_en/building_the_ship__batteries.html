Batteries

Energy is stored in size E batteries. (The "E" is for "E-normous".) Each battery component holds either 2 or 3 batteries .

Each battery  stores enough energy to power one use of a double cannon, a shield, or a double engine.

Battery components can be anywhere on the ship. They do not have to be next to the component they are powering.

To get the most from your ship, you’ll need double cannons and double engines, which means you will want as many batteries as possible.
