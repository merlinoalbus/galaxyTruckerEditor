FAQ

Can I decide not to use a simple engine or simple cannon?

No. Simple engines, simple cannons, and bonuses for aliens are always used automatically. The only thing you can decide is whether to use double engines and double cannons. And shields of course. If it costs a battery , you decide. Otherwise, it's automatic.

Can I shoot a big meteor with a cannon in the middle of my ship (not on the edge)?

Yes. Your cannon must be pointed in the right direction and be in the same column as the meteor (against a big meteor coming from ahead or behind) or in the same row or one of the two adjacent rows (against a big meteor coming from the side). Your cannon will not harm other components of your ship. That's why you keep the square in front of the barrel empty.

What if I have exposed connectors inside my ship?

If your ship has a hole in the middle, connectors adjacent to the hole still count as exposed connectors. They are protected from small meteors, however, because the meteor cannot strike them.

Can I decide which goods to give up?

No.

But in the board game—

This is not the board game. Whenever your cargo is taken, the goods and batteries are selected by the game. You have no say in the matter.

So what about crew members? When I have to lose crew, can I choose which ones I give up?

Yes. When giving up crew, you decide whether to give up aliens or humans and which cabins they will come from.

Can I rearrange cargo, crew, and batteries at any time?

No. The only things you can rearrange are blocks of goods, and these can be rearranged only when you gain new ones.
